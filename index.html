The Story

Jx Yvng

Ogheneaga Joy Eguono · 18 · Warri, Delta State

My name is Ogheneaga Joy Eguono, professionally known as Jx Yvng. I'm an 18-year-old artist from Warri, Delta State, Nigeria, born on February 12, 2008.

I'm a versatile musician, but my sound is deeply rooted in Emotional Rap, where I transform real-life experiences, pain, growth, and personal struggles into music that connects with people on a deeper level.

My breakthrough came on TikTok with the release of "Broken In My Prime", a song that resonated with thousands of listeners and helped me gain recognition across the platform. Through consistency, creativity, and daily content, I've continued to build a strong and loyal community of supporters.

My fanbase is known as the JXEEZ — a family of believers who support not only my music but also the vision, dream, and future we're building together. They're called Believers because they believe in me, my journey, and the impact we're destined to make.

This is more than music — it's a movement, a story, and a family growing stronger every day.
